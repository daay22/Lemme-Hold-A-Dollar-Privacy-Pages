Privacy Policy for “Lemme Hold a Dolla”

“Lemme Hold a Dolla” does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

This app is a comedic application that allows users to optionally send a $1.00 tip using Stripe’s secure payment system.

Information Collection

The app itself does not collect any personal information, analytics, or tracking data.

If a user chooses to send a payment, all payment information is handled directly by Stripe. We do not see, store, or process any credit card or personal payment information.

Please refer to Stripe’s Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data:
https://stripe.com/privacy

External Links

The app may open external links to Cash App or Venmo. Any data shared on those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.
